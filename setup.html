Set up your AI Vault

One line, on your Windows PC. It connects your vault to Claude (or Codex) by itself — nothing to click, no folder to choose.

  • A Windows PC
  • Claude or Codex already installed
  • The line we texted you
What you're getting A folder of your business knowledge, on this PC. It stays in step with your private server automatically — no cloud, nothing on the internet.
How Claude reaches it We add one entry to Claude's own list of tools, so it can open that folder. We don't change your settings or install any plugins.
What it can and can't do It reads your notes and can add new ones. It can never delete or overwrite anything, and it can't touch your confirmed facts or original SOPs.

Curious how it all fits together? There's a one-page map here: How the AI Vault works. Otherwise, just follow the three steps below.

1

Open PowerShell

Press the ⊞ Windows key, type powershell, then press Enter. A dark blue window opens — that's exactly right.

2

Paste the line we sent you

Right-click inside the blue window to paste the line AgentEA texted you, then press Enter. Now leave it alone and watch. It pulls your vault down and connects it to Claude for you — you don't click anything.

powershell ... -Label 'you' -Token '••••••••••••' ...
Most people Paste it into PowerShell

Exactly as shown above. Right-click to paste, press Enter, and leave it to run.

If you use Codex Let your AI do it

Rather not touch a terminal? Paste this into Codex instead, with your line at the end:

Please set up my AI Vault. Follow the instructions here: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AgentEA-AUS/ai-vault-install/main/AI-SETUP.md My personal invite line is: (paste the line from your file)
Your line has a one-time code in it — it works once, only for your computer. There's a chunky download in the middle (a minute or two), so give it a moment. If Windows asks, choose Yes or Run.
3

Reopen Claude, then ask it something

If Claude was already open, close it completely and open it again so it picks up your vault. (Usually the installer restarts it for you.) Then start a new chat and ask a real question about the business.

Using Codex instead? Nothing more to do — it was connected in step 2. Just run codex and ask.
All set

That's it — you're connected.

Your vault keeps itself up to date, and you can ask Claude to save notes back into it. You never reinstall.
