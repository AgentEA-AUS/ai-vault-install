# AI Vault installer

Connects your Mac and Claude Desktop to your AgentEA business brain.

Your AgentEA invite message contains the one line to paste into Terminal.
It sets up a private, receive-only copy of your business vault on your Mac
and installs the AI Vault extension into Claude Desktop.

- Nothing on your Mac is uploaded anywhere; your vault stays private.
- Every new computer must carry a valid one-time invite from AgentEA.
- Questions? Contact AgentEA.
